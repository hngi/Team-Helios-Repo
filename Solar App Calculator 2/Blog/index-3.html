Solar panel reliability: How reliable is solar power?

Oct 3, 2019 by Azeez Riqah

The question of solar panel reliability has been around since the start of the industry itself. You could say that it’s always there, casting a cloud over solar power.

What exactly are these concerns? Well, they take 2 main forms: concerns over performance of physical solar panels, and questions over solar power as a dependable source of energy. These reliability concerns were quite valid in the early days of solar panel adoption. But the fact of the matter is — as we’ll discuss in this article — these issues have been solved through advancements in technology and policy.
